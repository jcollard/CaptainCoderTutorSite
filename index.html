Captain Coder's Academy

One on one training for anyone!

Joseph Collard, aka Captain Coder, is a passionate educator, software engineer, and researcher who has been teaching Computer Science courses from elementary to college for over 10 years.

Not only does the Captain have a background in education but also has over 15 years of experience working in industry for startups, large companies, and has even served as a CTO.

Captain Coder develops and customizes one-on-one lessons to help students learn and master programming at their level.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Will STEM CT be open during the COVID-19 quarantine?

    Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, STEM CT is currently not holding in person classes. However, we will continue to bring the youth of Connecticut STEM based education through remote learning tools. Our instructors are working hard to adjust their courses to meet the current situation and we intend to launch online courses starting May 4th. To express interest in the courses you would like to see offered, please complete our Survey.

  • How will classes be adjusted until the lockdown is over?

    Surprisingly, each course has only required minor adjustments. Utilizing remote classroom tools such as Zoom for instruction and remote desktop control, instructors will be able to deliver the same intimate educational experience they provide in person.

  • How will my child get the resources necessary for their class?

    Prior to the start of the course, each student will receive a STEM CT laptop with all necessary software and resources to complete the course. This allows the student to sit down and begin learning immediately at the start of each lesson.

  • In the event that students can return to normal classes, what measures will be taken to keep students safe?

    STEM CT values our staff and students safety above anything else. To help keep everyone healthy and safe, we will be implementing the following policies: * 8 student maximum per class providing students with ample distance from each other * Computers, screens, keyboards, mice, and desks will be wiped down before and after every session * Hand sanitizer will be made available for every student The full policy can be read here.

  • Will my child have access to all materials necessary to complete the course?

    Absolutely. STEM CT courses are largely open ended to allow students to showcase their own personality and creativity within the projects they create. Because of this, each course comes with materials not only for the lessons and project they will complete each day but materials that go beyond what would be possible to complete during a single course. At STEM CT we believe life long learning is incredibly important and we want to ensure learning doesn't stop at the door.

  • Will my child be able to contact their instructors outside of class hours to have questions answered?

    For the duration of the course, students will have access to an online discussion board where they can send private messages to their instructor as well as post questions and messages for their classmates. For privacy and protection, instructors are not allowed to give their contact information directly to students without written permission from a parent or guardian. If a parent or guardian would like to make a written request for their instructors contact information, they may do so.

  • Where do your instructors come from?

    Our instructors are hand selected professionals who have not only worked with industry technology but also are incredible educators. They have a wide variety of backgrounds including working with Facebook, Google, Sandia National Labs, Los Alamos National Labs, and more!

  • What course do you recommend for my student?

    Every child is unique and we know just how important it is to choose an exciting and engaging course for them. Currently, STEM CT offers courses for two age groups: 10 - 12 and 13 - 17. As well as a wide range of options from educational game play to structured applied mathematics. We would love to talk to you about which of our courses is the right fit for your child. Do not hesitate to call (475) 231-5776 or email us contact@learnstemct.com.
